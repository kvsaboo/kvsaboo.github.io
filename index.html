Biography

I am a PhD candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. I am a part of the DEPEND group at CSL and my thesis advisor is Prof. Ravishankar K. Iyer. My research is on using machine learning to understand neurological disorders and to develop tools to diagnosis and treat them. I also work with Dr. Gregory Worrell (Mayo Clinic), Prof. Michal Kucewicz (Mayo Clinic), Dr. Prashanthi Vemuri (Mayo Clinic), and Dr. Jasmohan Bajaj (VCU).

I completed by integrated BTech and Mtech from Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay in Electrical Engineering with specialisation in Communications and Signal Processing. My thesis was with Prof. Vivek Borkar on topics in semi-supervised learning and resource allocation.
